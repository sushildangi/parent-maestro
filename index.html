Who are we?

  • We are Fort bend ISD parents and software engineers.
  • Enrolled our kids in extended learning, after school activities, and summer camps.
  • Experienced all the frictions in selecting the right camps and courses for kids.
  • Surveyed 100+ parents and interviewed 20+ parents to understand the problems in kids' activities selection.
  • Interviewed 10+ Entrepreneurs to understand their problems.
  • Want to help parents by removing friction in kids' activities selection.
  • Want to work with the extended learning team to help parents.
  • Want to collaborate with Fort bend ISD and help raise money for enterprise funds.

Parents

Research Findings

Parents Circumstances

  • Want best of best for children
  • Want best value for Money
  • No time in the busy schedule
  • Want Peace of Mind
  • Word of Mouth is main source of information
  • Don’t know all the options available

Obstacles

  • Too much effort – looking online, reviews and booking
  • Pro-active planning – with other parents
  • Too many comms (Email Fatigue)

Social/Emotional

  • Want Children to be happy
  • Proud of Children’s achievement
  • Want to be successful parent
Entrepreneurs

Research Findings

Entrepreneurs Circumstances

  • Enrollment is mainly through Word of mouth
  • Hard to track Marketing ROI – Google,Facebook
  • Parents’ Engagement requires a lot of effort

Obstacles

  • No tools to measure Parent’s/children's Engagement.
  • Lack of knowledge of modern technology
  • High cost of technology and marketing
  • Steep learning curve and no time

Social/Emotional

  • Want to be known as Success and Profitable entrepreneur
  • Want to have Financial stable life
  • Want to be known for Business Expansion and Community Contribution

How this works?

It takes a village to raise a child. But it takes an ISD to raise a child with full potential.

How we want to work?

ParentMaestro will work with ISD in each phase to achieve successful outcomes.

Example 1

  • Surveys the parents to get information on the interested activity types.
  • Analyze the survey data and understand the top two or three activity types.
               
  • Discuss with local Entrepreneurs and agree on the approach to work with them.
  • Build the activities on the mobile app and the support process.
  • Agree on the success criteria – How many children will join etc. Launch the activities.
  • Analyze the enrollment data vs. success criteria. Repeat with other activity types.

Example 2

  • Surveys the parents to get information on the interested Summer Camps and frictions.
               
  • Discuss with Summer Camps and agree on the approach to work with them.
  • Build the activities on the mobile app and the support process. Remove frictions.
  • Agree on the success criteria – How many summer camps sold etc. Launch.
  • Analyze the sold camp numbers vs. success criteria.

Example 3

  • Discuss with entrepreneurs and agree on the approach to work with them.
  • Build the activities on the mobile app and the support process. Remove frictions.
  • Agree on the success criteria – How many Birthday parties are sold etc. Launch.
  • Recommendation to the Parents based on the Children's DOB and wish list.
               
  • Analyze the sold numbers vs. success criteria.

Example 4

  • Discuss with entrepreneurs and agree on the approach to work with them
  • Build the Marketing activities on the mobile app and the support process. Remove frictions by building a call-to-action (enrollments, booking etc.) button.
  • Agree on the success criteria – How many Call- to-action tasks are sold etc. Launch.
  • Recommendation to the Parents based on the wish list and other criteria.
               
  • Analyze the call-to-action numbers vs. success criteria.
